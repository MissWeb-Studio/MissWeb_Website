Free-Tier Deploy

Designed specifically for rapid prototyping, digital resumes, portfolio showcases, or zero-budget startups. Under this agreement, you exclusively pay for our development labor, completely bypassing monthly recurring infrastructure charges.

Visual Presentation: Your final production website will look identical, operate just as beautifully, and keep the exact same clean design rules as any premium enterprise website. The only difference is the address and platform restrictions under the hood.

  • Technical Stack Limits: The platform relies strictly on the non-paid public ecosystems of Vercel Plans ,GitHub Pages Architecture or Render Plans . As a consequence, you will not have a generic standalone dot-com url. Instead, your brand is linked directly to the hosting extension (e.g., yourbrand.vercel.app or yourname.github.io).
  • Performance Behaviors: Sites can experience minor latency issues known as cold-starts . If your platform receives no visitors for an extended period, the underlying free server sleeps. The next incoming user will experience a temporary 2 to 5-second delay while the environment boots back up. Bandwidth limits and file uploads are rigidly capped by the provider.
  • Maintenance Restrictions: Hand-off is immediate upon deployment. No ongoing security monitors, core software upgrades, or text alterations are provided by Miss Web without transitioning to a dedicated hourly development invoice.

Self-Managed

A solid blueprint for established startups or small businesses that want an absolute professional web presence, a dedicated URL, and zero server caps, but possess the internal operational capacity to process their own server billing and system maintenance.

Infrastructure Cost Breakdown

Premium Hosting Scalability: Your real-world monthly costs directly correlate to user traffic volume and app design complexity. For standard informational profiles or local trade portals, you can easily secure trusted, cheap web hosting starting around +-R100 monthly.

Example Complexities: If you decide to scale into an extensive Multi-vendor Marketplace or a complex Real Estate portal handling thousands of active concurrent image searches, data demands increase. Hosting prices would likely scale to +-R350 or up to R1,200+ monthly for a Virtual Private Server (VPS). For reliable web setups, we use trusted providers like HostAfrica or local environments such as Xneelo.

Domain Extension Variations: To route visitors to your site, you will need to buy a domain. Extensions feature vastly different price tags. A typical South African localized extension (.co.za) or common business choices sit stably at around +-R120 yearly.

Example Extensions: Standard regional paths like .co.za run cheap at R100–R150/year. Global target paths such as .com or tech variants like .net often sit around R250 to R400+ yearly. Premium boutique endings like .ai or .studio can easily scale past R1,500/year. You can buy these via top domain registrars like Domains.co.za.

  • Free Tier Option: Users have the flexibility to pair a custom domain with free hosting, or pair a premium hosting plan with a platform-provided subdomain. Combining a free hosting service with a free domain registration is not supported; such configurations are exclusively reserved for the full Free Tier package.
  • System Handover: Upon full project sign-off, our technical team safely moves all domain registrars and third-party host control panels over to your identity, directly linking your personal payment card.
  • Operational Support: Miss Web steps back post-launch. Future debugging, server errors, or theme amendments require a clean work order evaluated under our standard hourly development pricing.

Miss Web Managed

Our signature white-glove "Subscription Hub". We take over technical administration, coordinate server updates, monitor firewall defense grids, and deploy text or asset modifications so your focus remains on expanding operations.

Maintenance Subscription Formula & Caps

The entry base price for this managed service rests at R550 monthly. This flat fee is explicitly divided to allow Miss Web to systematically handle your domain subscription monthly on your behalf along with your recurring premium hosting subscription fees.

R550 (Base Maintenance Fee) - R100 (Allocated Hosting Cap) - (R120 Domain Cap / 12 Months) = R440 Core Miss Web Management Profit

Variable Cap Regulations: If you select/your website is in need of high-demand system paths or specialized domain extensions that exceed our standard limits, the overage is cleanly added to your monthly R550 base billing.

  • Standard Domain Cap: Up to R120 / Year
  • Standard Hosting Cap: Up to R100 / Month

Practical Example: If your custom brand strategy demands a premium domain running at R200/year (a R80 annual addition over our cap) and premium resource-heavy cloud hosting priced at R250/month (a R150 monthly addition over our cap), the accumulated variations flow over. Your modified monthly maintenance fee will increase exactly by R230, bringing your complete monthly total to R780 instead of R550.

To find or research these items yourself, you can cross-check premium packages directly via HostAfrica and Domains.co.za.

  • Free Tier Option: Users have the flexibility to pair a custom domain with free hosting, or pair a premium hosting plan with a platform-provided subdomain. Combining a free hosting service with a free domain registration is not supported; such configurations are exclusively reserved for the full Free Tier package.
  • Maintanance Fee Regarding Free-Tier Option: Either Option between Domain or Hosting selected as Free-Tier will reduce maintainance fees based on the free option selected.
  • Content Allocations: Each month, you receive up to 2 hours of rapid designer revisions (swapping text copy, correcting office numbers, changing team profile pictures, adding features or refining the website). Hours do not roll over.
  • Asset Ownership Protection: Despite Miss Web functioning as your technical administrator, you retain 100% legal ownership of your data and intellectual assets. You can transition back to a self-managed framework with 30 days notice.
