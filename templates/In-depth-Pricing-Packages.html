Free-Tier Deploy

Designed specifically for rapid prototyping, digital resumes, portfolio showcases, or zero-budget startups. Under this agreement, you exclusively pay for our development labor, completely bypassing monthly recurring infrastructure charges.

Visual Presentation: Your final production website will look identical, operate just as beautifully, and keep the exact same clean design rules as any premium enterprise website. The only difference is the address and platform restrictions under the hood.

  • Technical Stack Limits: The platform relies strictly on the non-paid public ecosystems of Vercel Plans ,GitHub Pages or Render Plans . As a consequence, you will not have a generic standalone dot-com url. Instead, your brand is linked directly to the hosting extension (e.g., yourbrand.vercel.app).
  • Performance Behaviors: Sites can experience minor latency issues known as cold-starts . If your platform receives no visitors for an extended period, the underlying free server sleeps.
  • Maintenance Restrictions: Hand-off is immediate upon deployment. No ongoing security monitors, core software upgrades, or text alterations are provided by Miss Web without transitioning to a dedicated hourly invoice.

Self-Managed

A solid blueprint for established startups or small businesses that want an absolute professional web presence, a dedicated URL, and zero server caps, but possess the internal operational capacity to process their own server billing and system maintenance.

Infrastructure Cost Breakdown

Premium Hosting Scalability: Your real-world monthly costs directly correlate to user traffic volume and app design complexity. For standard informational profiles or local trade portals, you can easily secure trusted, cheap web hosting starting around +-R100 monthly.

Example Complexities: If you decide to scale into an extensive Multi-vendor Marketplace or a complex Real Estate portal handling thousands of active concurrent image searches, data demands increase. Hosting prices would likely scale to +-R350 or up to R1,200+ monthly for a VPS.

Domain Extension Variations: To route visitors to your site, you will need to buy a domain. Extensions feature vastly different price tags. A typical South African localized extension (.co.za) or common business choices sit stably at around +-R120 yearly.

  • Free Tier Option: Users have the flexibility to pair a custom domain with free hosting, or pair a premium hosting plan with a platform-provided subdomain.
  • System Handover: Upon full project sign-off, our technical team safely moves all domain registrars and third-party host control panels over to your identity.
  • Operational Support: Miss Web steps back post-launch. Future debugging, server errors, or theme amendments require a clean work order.

Miss Web Managed

Our signature white-glove "Subscription Hub". We take over technical administration, coordinate server updates, monitor firewall defense grids, and deploy text or asset modifications so your focus remains on expanding operations.

Maintenance Subscription Formula

The entry base price for this managed service rests at R550 monthly. This flat fee is explicitly divided to allow Miss Web to systematically handle your domain subscription monthly on your behalf along with your recurring premium hosting fees.

R550 (Base Maintenance Fee) - R100 (Allocated Hosting Cap) - (R120 Domain Cap / 12 Months) = R440 Core Miss Web Management Profit

Variable Cap Regulations: If you select high-demand system paths or specialized domain extensions that exceed our standard limits, the overage is cleanly added to your monthly R550 base billing.

  • Standard Domain Cap: Up to R120 / Year
  • Standard Hosting Cap: Up to R100 / Month
  • Content Allocations: Each month, you receive up to 2 hours of rapid designer revisions (swapping text copy, correcting office numbers, changing team profile pictures, adding features). Hours do not roll over.
  • Asset Ownership Protection: Despite Miss Web functioning as your technical administrator, you retain 100% legal ownership of your data and intellectual assets.
