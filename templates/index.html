Domain Name & Hosting

Domain Name: Think of it as your website's street address. Just like your home needs a unique address so people can find you, your website needs a domain name. Example: www.yourbusiness.com

Hosting Platform: Think of it as the land where your website's house is built. It's the online space that stores all your website files, images, and content so that anyone with internet access can visit your site 24/7.
